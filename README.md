# KalkulatorPajak

  paragraph 3 (a).

### Tarif

* Individual Income Tax based on Taxable Income on Article 17
  paragraph 1 (a).
* Corporate Income Tax based on Taxable Income on Article 17
  paragraph 1 (b).

### Form Calculation

* Super Simple Individual Income Tax Form Calculation (1770-SS).
